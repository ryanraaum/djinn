This site provides three web-based utilities (seq2sites, sites2seq, and haplotype) useful to studies of human mitochondrial DNA sequences.

seq2sites

seq2sites (sequence to sites) analyzes human mtDNA sequence data in order to determine the polymorphic sites relative to the rCRS.

sites2seq

sites2seq (sites to sequence) transforms polymorphic site information into sequence data.

haplotype

haplotype compares mtDNA polymorphisms identified in sequence data to a database of haplotypes in order to infer a likely haplogroup.
